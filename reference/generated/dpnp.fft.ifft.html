dpnp.fft.ifft¶

dpnp.fft.ifft(x1, n=None, axis=- 1, norm=None)[source]¶

Compute the one-dimensional inverse discrete Fourier Transform.

Limitations

Parameter norm is unsupported. Parameter x1 supports dpnp.int32, dpnp.int64, dpnp.float32, dpnp.float64 and dpnp.complex128 datatypes only.

For full documentation refer to numpy.fft.ifft.
